Welcome!

Welcome to the About Us page of AI Deepfake Awareness. This website was created as an independent project by a final-year student passionate about digital literacy and the responsible use of artificial intelligence. The main purpose of the site is to raise awareness about the risks and realities of deepfakes, empowering users to recognize, understand, and respond to AI-generated manipulated content.

Motivation

The motivation behind this website stems from the rapidly increasing difficulty of detecting AI-generated deepfakes. As deepfake technology advances, it is becoming harder for both automated tools and humans to distinguish between real and manipulated content, with recent studies showing that even advanced detection systems can see accuracy drop by up to 50% in real-world scenarios. With the number of deepfake incidents rising dramatically each year, education is more crucial than ever. While detection technology continues to evolve, it cannot keep pace with new manipulations alone. That’s why combining education with technological solutions is essential - by raising awareness and providing practical knowledge, we empower individuals to question what they see, verify information, and stay safe in an era where digital trust is under constant threat.
